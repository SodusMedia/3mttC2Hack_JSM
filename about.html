why choose us?

JSM offers FREE courses for secondary school students to kick-start their learning journey into the world of technology, powered with generative AI search. Also allow volunteers to share their knowledge.

Our Vision

To empower the Nigerian youth through learning, job creation and opportunities.

Our Mission

The mission of JSM: create a safe place for youths to be productive with acquired tech skills.

Who are we?

JSM is an initiative of 3mtt cohort 2 hackathon participants (LearnMakers team) tagged TechForGood Inspired by Dr. 'Bosun Tijani's 3mtt platform, who is the honourable Minister of Communications, Innovation and Digital Economy of the Federal Republic of Nigeria and Chairman of the Board of the USPF.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access a Course?

You have to be logged in to access the course content.

Can I get Certificates?

Yes, upon completion of a course including the project, you would be issued a certificate.

Are the Courses for Free?

Yes, all courses are free of cost.

Can I get a Job from here?

No, we do not offer that as of now, but we ensure you are equipped for the Job market, and provide recommendations.

What can I do with this platform?

By taking JSM Courses, you will be equipped with tech skills ready for Job offers.

Will I find my secondary school classmates?

Yes, once you refer them, and they register!

Can I work on a project?

Certainly, you would work on Projects to put what you have learnt to practice.
